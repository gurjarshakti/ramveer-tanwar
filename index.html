RamveerTanwar

Environmentalist

Mr. Ramveer Tanwar, who is an Environmentalist had been passionate about water conservation during his Student life. During his graduate years, Mr. Tanwar had begun a campaign Jal Chaupal in Gautam Buddha Nagar district of Uttar Pradesh, urging people to conserve water and to save natural resources like ponds and lakes. Ramveer got training in Central pollution Control board, MOEF, Govt. of India. For conservation of Groundwater, Ramveer Tanwar started the cleaning and rejuvenation process of the lakes and ponds. Since 2014, Mr. Tanwar and team has restored more than12 ponds/lakes in Uttar Pradesh and also now guiding the peoples in all over India for doing same. Right now He is working in association with Different Corporate and NGOs.

Projects

Jal Chaupal

This is an Awareness campaign called Jal Chaupal to discuss water conservation in villages, Schools, Colleges. Issues such as garbage disposal and lake encroachments are discussed in these gatherings.

Project Jal

Project Jal works for water conservation and management, rainwater harvesting and rejuvenates and create new water bodies PAN India in Expertise of Environmentalist Ramveer Tanwar. Our objective is to conserve and maintain the hydrological cycle of India and rejuvenate depleting water table. Project Jal is joint nonprofit initiative of Green Yatra, Ramveer Tanwar and SayTrees.

#SelfieWithPond

We started #SelfieWithPond online campaign to connect the youngsters and people to their nearby water bodies. So peoples can discuss about dying water bodies and can be motivated to ponds/lakes.We connected with 100,000 youths from Pan India as well as 9 other countries.
